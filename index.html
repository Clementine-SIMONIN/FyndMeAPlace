Find me a Place is an application that allows anyone to publish stories about the places where they are and show other users the atmosphere of the place. Noise pollution, population intensity ,festive or calm atmosphere there is something for everyone. Our goal is to allow people to feel good in the places where they go they have found places that correspond to their preferences and fashions of the day.
